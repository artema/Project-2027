<P><JC>International arms show in St. Petersburg</JC>
<P>
<P>
<P>The city is getting ready for a big event! In just two days an international contemporary military technologies and arms show will take place! The exhibition will present the newest developments of Russian and foreign scientists in heavy-engineering and arms manufacturing. The following counties are listed as participants: USA, Germany, Austria, England, Sweden, and China. 
<P>The show will be open during the whole of next week and will conclude in a large-scale military maneuvers in "almost-real-world" conditions!
