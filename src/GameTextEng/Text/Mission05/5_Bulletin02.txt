<P>Russian Confederation Governmental regulatios #1048, from 19.10.2027.
<P>
<P>"...Forbid the RC law enforcement officials to open fire at the unidentified and suspicious-looking persons. The RC LEO are entitled to open fire only in emergency cases (i.e. self-defense). The effective fire is only allowed by the order of commander or another higher authority.
<P>During the curfew the unidentified and suspicious-looking persons should be stopped and checked, their personality identified, and the report of the event should be made and presented to the commander or another higher authority."
